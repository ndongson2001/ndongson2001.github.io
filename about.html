Who I Am

My name is Ndong Peter Maum. I am an educator, community advocate, and development practitioner from Cameroon. My work focuses on education, digital inclusion, and community empowerment.

I have experience working with learners and communities to improve access to knowledge, technology, and opportunities—especially in underserved areas.

This website documents my journey, initiatives, and projects aimed at creating positive social impact.
